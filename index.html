Kevin Yang

yangk at berkeley dot edu // Scholar // GitHub // Curriculum Vitae



I am a 5th-year PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Dan Klein, within Berkeley NLP and BAIR.

I am broadly interested in structured planning, control, and alignment/instruction-tuning methods for improving the quality and safety of natural language generation, especially for long context.
